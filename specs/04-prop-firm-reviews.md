## Standard Prop Firm Review Structure

```
1. Hero Section
   - Firm name, rating
   - Challenge price highlight
   - Max funding amount
   - Payout split

2. Quick Navigation

3. Overview + AnalystNotes
   - What is [Firm]?
   - History and reputation
   - Unique selling points

4. Challenge Rules + AnalystNotes
   - Profit targets
   - Drawdown limits (daily/overall)
   - Time limits
   - Trading rules (news, weekends, etc.)
   - Comparison table: Phase 1 vs Phase 2

5. Account Sizes & Pricing
   - All account options
   - Price per size
   - Value comparison

6. Payout Structure + AnalystNotes
   - Profit split percentages
   - Payout frequency
   - Scaling plan
   - Withdrawal methods

7. Platforms & Tools
   - Supported platforms
   - Instruments available
   - Leverage offered

8. Pros & Cons Grid

9. User Reviews
   - Trustpilot integration
   - Real trader feedback

10. FAQ Section
    - Challenge-specific questions
    - Payout questions
    - Rules clarification

11. Final Verdict + AnalystNotes
```
