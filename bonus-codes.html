Get answers to common questions about trading bonuses, partner codes, and how to maximize your offers.

How do trading bonus codes work?

Trading bonus codes are special promotional codes that brokers offer through partnerships. When you sign up using our exclusive codes, you get access to enhanced bonuses, better terms, or exclusive features that aren't available to regular signups.

Are these bonus codes legitimate?

Yes, all our bonus codes are 100% legitimate and verified partnerships with regulated brokers. We only work with reputable brokers that are licensed by major financial authorities like FCA, ASIC, CySEC, and others.

Do I need to deposit immediately to get the bonus?

Most bonuses require a minimum deposit to activate, but the specific requirements vary by broker. Some offer demo account bonuses, while others require real money deposits. Always check the specific terms for each offer.

Can I withdraw the bonus immediately?

Bonus funds typically come with trading requirements (wagering requirements) that must be met before withdrawal. This means you need to trade a certain volume before you can withdraw the bonus. Profits from trading with bonus funds are usually withdrawable once requirements are met.

What happens if the code doesn't work?

If a code doesn't work, contact the broker's support team immediately during the signup process. Alternatively, reach out to us and we'll help resolve the issue. Most brokers can apply codes retroactively if contacted quickly.
