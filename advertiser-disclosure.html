Our Commitment to Transparency

At TradeTheDay, we believe in complete transparency about how we fund our operations and maintain this free resource for traders worldwide. This page explains our relationships with brokers and exchanges, and how we ensure our reviews remain honest and unbiased.

How We Make Money

TradeTheDay receives compensation from some of the brokers and exchanges featured on our website. This compensation comes in several forms:

  • Affiliate Commissions: We earn a commission when you sign up with a broker through our links and meet certain trading criteria
  • CPA (Cost Per Acquisition): Some partners pay us a fixed fee for qualified new customers
  • Revenue Sharing: Occasionally, we participate in revenue-sharing agreements based on trading volume
  • Advertising: Display advertising and sponsored content clearly marked as such

Important: You do NOT pay anything extra when signing up through our links. In many cases, we negotiate exclusive bonuses or better terms for our readers.

Editorial Independence

Our editorial content is produced independently of our business relationships. Here's how we maintain objectivity:

Separation of Teams

Our content team operates independently from our partnerships team, ensuring reviews aren't influenced by commercial relationships.

Consistent Methodology

All brokers are evaluated using the same criteria and scoring system, regardless of partnership status.

Negative Reviews

We publish critical reviews of partners when warranted. Poor service is called out regardless of commercial relationships.

User Feedback

Real user reviews and feedback are incorporated into our ratings and cannot be influenced by brokers.

What This Means For You

  • Free Access: Our compensation model allows us to provide all content, tools, and resources completely free to you
  • Better Deals: We often negotiate exclusive bonuses and promotions for our readers
  • Honest Reviews: Commercial relationships don't guarantee positive reviews - we call out issues regardless
  • Your Choice: We present multiple options and let you decide what's best for your trading needs
  • No Pressure: We never push you toward any specific broker - the choice is always yours

Identifying Partnerships

While we may have commercial relationships with many brokers we review, not all links are affiliate links. We aim to be transparent about partnerships through:

  • This disclosure page, which you're reading now
  • Footer disclaimers on all pages
  • Clear labeling of sponsored content when applicable
  • Disclosure statements within reviews where relevant

Questions About Our Policies?

We welcome questions about our disclosure policies and business model. Transparency is core to our mission of helping traders make informed decisions.
