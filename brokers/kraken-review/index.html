Based in San Francisco, the exchange is known for its robust security, regulatory compliance, and transparent operations including regular Proof of Reserves audits. Kraken serves millions of users across 190+ countries.

Frequently Asked Questions

Kraken is considered one of the safest crypto exchanges. Founded in 2011, it has never been hacked. The exchange offers Proof of Reserves audits, cold storage, 2FA, and advanced security features. It's regulated by FinCEN in the US and FCA in the UK.

Kraken charges 0.16% maker and 0.26% taker fees for spot trading (Kraken Pro). Instant Buy has higher fees around 1.5%. Staking is free to set up with rewards varying by asset. Volume discounts apply for higher tiers.

Kraken has no minimum for crypto deposits. For fiat, minimums vary by currency and method - typically around $1-10 for most options. Bank transfers may have higher minimums depending on your country.
